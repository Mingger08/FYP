Dashboard


    What You Can Do?
Manage Company

Manage company information such as name, address, name of person in-charge, position and contact details.


Manage Business

Manage business information such as category, zone, premise location and contract date by adding, editing and deleting.


Manage Employee

Manage employee and their personal information by adding, editing and deleting.

Add Complaint

Write a complaint in order to seek for relevant unit to solve problems like broken lights, sanitation and other issue.
Add Report

Write and submit any report of settlement of issue or complaint in a more detailed form to relevant authorities.


Manage Profile

Manage personal information such as name, email, contact number, gender so to differenciate the user account.
